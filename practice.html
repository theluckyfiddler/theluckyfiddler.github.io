Is my young child ready?

Practice, Practice, Practice

At some point, most parents find it difficult to get their child to practice. Some things to consider:

  • Are you listening enough?
  • Keep practice time short.
  • Start with something easy; end with something fun.
  • If something is too frustrating, cut your losses.
  • Consider practicing at a different time of day.
  • Make sure your are listening!

Here are some things I thought might be helpful that I found on a web search. Of course you can do your own search and cater it to your specific situation. I recommend doing some of it before practice becomes difficult.
