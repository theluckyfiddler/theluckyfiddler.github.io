Is my young child ready?

Welcome to the adventure that is learning to play the violin or viola. Please explore my website to help you decide if I would be a good teacher for you or your child. My goal as a teacher is to help my students to play very well, in a fun and thoughtful way.

  • I am a Suzuki teacher; I also teach fiddling.
  • I live in Northwest Omaha.
  • I have space in my studio now.
  • A 30 minute lesson costs $20 in my living room.
  • A 30 minute lesson costs $25-30 at your Omaha area location.
  • I accept students from about age 3 to adult.
